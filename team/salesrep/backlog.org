clock.

** Task template
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       f75aace7-57e5-41fe-a8a9-443d23bb5ae2
:END:

A task is defined by the answers to, at least, these questions:

- What does it mean for this task to be "done"?
- Why is this task worth doing?
