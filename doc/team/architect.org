* Architect
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       87e740ec-6036-4154-968d-625196f90ee4
:END:

The Architect owns the *technical integrity*, *long-term maintainability*, and
*cost-efficiency* of the product while ensuring every technical decision supports what
the Sales Rep can sell and what the developers can reliably deliver.

- *Minimizes onboarding costs*. Guarantees excellent documentation, consistent
  patterns, and a clean codebase. See: [[ref:e4a12ee6-a0a1-4ffd-9031-28b4a00b5ca7][On-boarding]].

- *Understands the product*. Maintains complete knowledge of what the product does
  (business value) *and* how it does it. See: [[ref:94a78f42-9098-4796-90c1-725e20a07fbd][Architecture]].

- *Maximizes business value*. Collaborate with the Sales Rep to translate opportunities
  into feasible technical solutions with realistic estimates and cost ranges. See:
  [[ref:90abac79-d226-4de2-9174-9df6c48c25c0][Backlog]].

- *Minimizes Costs*. Reduce technical debt, automating repetitive tasks. See:
  [[ref:18351049-22e2-4f4e-ab27-50de6c76ca66][Infrastructure]].

- *Maximizes extensibility*. Adding new features, customers, or markets can be added
  with minimal cost.

- *Owns the development process*. See: [[ref:5513764b-8595-4e2d-bcf9-8e8214ee901b][Contributing]].
